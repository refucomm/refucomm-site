Our mission

To provide refugees with knowledge about their rights and their obligations and to understand administrative and legal processes. We believe that knowledge empowers refugees to make decisions and take back some control over their lives

About Refucomm

RefuComm is a humanitarian organisation created by a group of independent volunteers who work with refugees in Europe.

We were founded by Sharon Silvey and Florian in October 2015 from a field in Croatia.

We operate in some of the world’s most vulnerable places. Our work is concentrated in the areas of caring for women, children and unaccompanied minors. We provide much needed assistance to individuals and communities that have been affected by war, conflict and the cycle of poverty.
